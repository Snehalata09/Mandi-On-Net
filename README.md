# Mandi On Net – Farmer-to-Customer Agricultural Marketplace

## Project Overview

**Mandi On Net** is a web-based agricultural marketplace designed to connect farmers directly with consumers, eliminating the involvement of middlemen and aggregators. The platform aims to improve farmers' profitability by enabling them to sell their produce at fair market prices while allowing customers to purchase fresh agricultural products at lower costs.

The project addresses one of the major challenges faced by farmers—receiving inadequate compensation for their produce due to long supply chains. By creating a transparent digital marketplace, Mandi On Net promotes fair trade, better pricing, and enhanced economic conditions for the farming community.

---

## Problem Statement

In traditional agricultural markets, farmers often receive lower profits because multiple intermediaries participate in the distribution process. This increases product prices for consumers while reducing earnings for farmers.

Mandi On Net provides a direct farmer-to-consumer platform that:

* Reduces dependency on intermediaries.
* Increases farmers' income.
* Offers consumers fresh products at competitive prices.
* Promotes digital transformation in agriculture.

## System Modules

### 1. Farmer Registration & Authentication

Farmers can register using personal and farming details. Registered users can securely log in and manage their products through a dedicated dashboard.

### 2. Customer Registration & Authentication

Customers create accounts to browse products, manage shopping carts, and place orders securely.

### 3. Product Management

Farmers can upload product information including:

* Product Name
* Category
* Quantity
* Price
* Product Images
* Product Description

### 4. Product Verification

Products undergo verification before being displayed to customers, ensuring authenticity and quality standards.

### 5. Shopping Cart & Order Management

Customers can:

* Add products to cart
* Manage quantities
* Review orders
* Complete purchases
